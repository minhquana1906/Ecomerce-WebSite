ABOUT

” Freakers was born around 2010/2011 under the name The Freakers. On October 19, 2013 we made it official with our first store opening in Hanoi. Nowadays, Freakers is into all sorts of cool stuff like skateboarding, music, fashion, graphic design, film and food. As a skateboarding-based brand, we’ve connected with many young and talented individuals and other like-minded folks, all with the goal of building a sick and wholesome skateboarding culture here in Vietnam. Our motto is to “Create value and grow together” – because that’s how we roll!”
